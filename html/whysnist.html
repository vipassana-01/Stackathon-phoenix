Why SNISTians?

The answer is straight and simple, Corporate firms are inclined to recruit from SNIST for the following reasons:

  1. We follow a stringent student selection procedure on the basis of merit.
  2. Our world class curriculum is constantly updated to match the industry requirements.
  3. Our highly qualified faculty members with doctoral degrees are actively involved in research and journal publications.
  4. Our Internship Programmes ensure that every student spends over 7 months working in the industry even before graduation ensuring real life applications of their course
  5. We nurture students in every field of their interest. Soft-skills development is an integral part of classroom experience through presentations and open-ended learning techniques.
  6. Students are given further avenues to express themselves and develop skills beyond academics through a variety of extracurricular activities including student managed clubs and events.
  7. Strong online presence Social networking has become the primary way that people communicate. But it is a double-edged sword
  8. Enthusiasm,initiative and consistent effort for betterment in every aspect to take initiative on the job, you can count on being noticed and rewarded.
  9. Good cultural fit the way we make different sorts of people to connect is far beyond the bars that one needed and ready to fit,work and excel in the industry
  10. Multitaskers who thrive on variety of projects such individuals demonstrates a passion for learning new things and enjoys a variety of work, chances that those individuals are ambitious and inquisitive -- two qualities that are critical to success and advancement."
